Our Extensive Tree Care Services

Our certified arborists understand the impact of the environment of each tree and how they react to it. We can help you maintain your trees from inside out and ensure that your trees are healthy and safe for the inhabitants in the vicinity. A thorough check will be made by our arborists on the visit, for all signs of damage or disease.

Soil Treatment

Since trees consume a lot of their nutrients from the soil, soil treatment can do wonders for your tree from the ground up. In soil treatment, we add organic matter rich in nutrients that will boost the growth of your tree and protect it from possible diseases and insects. Besides, the soil is already responsible for many tree ailments like yellowing of young leaves, weak branches, and stunted growth, among others. 

Our soil enhancers rebuild the soil's organic profile, and the trees readily absorb the nutrients, and they get nurtured inside out.

Disease Management

Irrespective of your love and care, all trees are susceptible to fall victim to tree disease. Common tree diseases are fungal growth, root rot, leaf spots, blight, cankers (stem disease), wilding needle cast, among others. 

However, each disease needs specific care and treatment. The treatment costs may vary depending on the extent of the disease and the tree type. 

Insect Management

We can come over and inspect the trees for diseases and pests or insect problems. If the tree is found to be infested, we will remove the pests and see that it does not become hazardous for your life and property.

This way, we will halt the further decay of the trees and remove infected limbs and branches so that it does not spread to the rest of the tree or plants.
