Our Stump Grinding Proccess

Gone are the days when 3 to 4 well-maintained guys chop down the stump to the last shred with nothing more than an ax and their strength. It would take these muscular men a considerable amount of time to remove every trace of the wood stump from your property. Piece by piece, they would take it all apart and replace the whole with mud and soil. 

We have come a long way from that time. The modern-day itinerary consists of using a stump removal machine. For the majority of the cases, a single tree removal expert is sufficient. This is how we plan to remove the upper part of the stump from the ground by taking technology’s help. 

  1. We evaluate the stump’s condition and its height. For ideal grinding, the stump must be as close to the ground as possible. If not, we will have to cut the stump with a chainsaw. Once it is within the correct range, we bring in the big boys toy, the Grinding Machine, or Stump Removal Machine.  
  2. Our stump removal machine is pre-fitted with carbide teeth on a rotator operated either by a remote or manually by the machine driver. Once the cutter starts grinding, it cuts the wood into small shreds or pieces. 
  3. The powerful and mechanized grinder takes apart 2 inches of the stump consistently. Since the whole process is mechanized, it won’t take more than 3 to 4 hours for removing the stump, provided the stump is not too big. 

Stump Removal Without Chemicals

It is strongly recommended by our tree doctors that you do not use any kind of chemicals to degrade the stump’s strength. Without an in-depth understanding of these toxic condiments and its effect on the wood and land, refrain from using any such thing. 

As a modern-age tree removal service can get rid of the stump without using any sort of chemical. Our process is safe, faster, and leads to a plain and smooth ground.
